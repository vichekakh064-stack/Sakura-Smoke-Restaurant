Our Story & Ambience

The origins and sublime ambiance of Sakura & Smoke

Welcome to a seamless fusion of culinary art and authentic Japanese nature.

At​​ Sakura & Smoke we focus not only on premium flavors, but we also craft a visual masterpiece and an ambiance that makes you feel as though you are unwinding in a traditional garden in Kyoto.

Adorned with vibrant, deep-red sakura trees stretching gracefully over a lively natural stone stream, and complemented by traditional red lanterns casting a warm, gentle glow, every meal here becomes a romantic and unforgettable memory.

Whether you are gathering with family, hosting business partners, or enjoying a special date, the experience of dining under the soft light of Japanese lanterns to the gentle soothing sound of flowing water will melt all your stress away, leaving nothing but pure joy."
